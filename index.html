About me

EDITOR.
CREATOR.
STORYTELLER.

I've been editing videos since 2020 — not because someone hired me to, but because I genuinely love what happens when you get the cut just right. What started as editing my own content became a real obsession with craft: pacing, rhythm, colour, motion.

Working exclusively in DaVinci Resolve, I handle everything from clean basic cuts to advanced sequences — speed ramps that hit like a punch, transitions that feel inevitable, and colour grades that give footage a mood of its own.

My personal content has crossed 300,000 views in total. That means I don't just know how to make videos look good — I know how to make videos people actually finish watching.

How I work

THE PROCESS

01
Understand the vision

I start by understanding what you want to say — the tone, the feel, the purpose. Good editing starts before the timeline.

02
Rough cut & structure

Best takes, strong narrative spine, pacing decisions — laying the foundation before any polish happens.

03
Motion & transitions

Speed ramps, cuts to beat, seamless transitions — this is where the video starts to feel alive and intentional.

04
Grade & deliver

A consistent colour grade ties everything together. Delivered export-ready in your required format.
